About Me

My name is Dominic Bradfute, I was born on April 24th, 1998 in San Jose, California. I moved to Texas about 2 years later, and I've lived here ever since. I enjoy video games, rock music, comedies, and all around good movies, music, and tv shows.


Some of my favorite video games are Skyrim, Bioshock, Destiny, Overwatch, Bloodborne, and Pokemon. My favorite genre of music is rock/hard rock, I listen to Disturbed, Seether, Shinedown, Hollywood Undead, Three Days Grace, Bullet For My Valentine, Five Finger Death Punch, Avenged Sevenfold, etc. I like shows ranging from comedies like South Park, Rick and Morty, and Its Always Sunny in Philadelphia, to more serious stuff like Breaking Bad.
